# Promo Studio Brand Rules

The base page is deliberately plain so the variant can show visible campaign work.

Keep these commerce facts unchanged:
- Price: $42.00
- SKU: RMT-001
- Inventory: 3
- Cart button behavior: clicking adds one tote to the local demo cart

Campaign variants may change layout, tone, color, messaging hierarchy, supporting sections, and component composition. Keep copy accurate, accessible, and specific to the Ribbed Market Tote.

Product image rule:
- Keep the original tote photo fully visible.
- Do not crop, stretch, filter, replace, or cover the product image.
- Badges and urgency labels may sit near the image frame, but must not obscure the tote.
